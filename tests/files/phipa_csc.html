Civil Service Commission

Services

Appeal to the Civil Service Commission
City employees can submit an appeal to the Civil Service Commission if they feel that wrongful action has been taken against them at work.

About

The Civil Service Commission advises the mayor and the director of human resources on personnel administration in City service. It upholds the interest of the City’s merit-based civil service system. which is vital to the public interest.

The commission:

  • Serves as an appellate tribunal for employee appeals.
  • Rules on proposed changes to the Civil Service Regulations and the Classification and Pay Plans.
  • Rules on requests for exemptions from civil service and waivers of the residency requirement.

The commission is a quasi-judicial body and spends most of its time on its appellate function. It conducts fact-finding public hearings on employee appeals. It also issues formal written decisions containing factual and legal conclusions.
